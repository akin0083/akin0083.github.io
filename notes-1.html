Html tags and their descriptions

img
The image tag makes websites engaging and helps with esthetics of the design.Images are used for displaying logos, products, team members, store and more.
a
the anchor tag is a paired tag and the content we want to display as a link is added between the opening and the closing .
form
the form tag is a paired tag and any information we want to collect using form will be added inside the opening
and closing
.
div
div is used to wrap around multiple child elements. it also divides the content into logical groups which will show the content on a separate line.
